What’s up! Welcome to the main explanation for “Control Your Body, Control Your Mind.” We are going to learn how to feel better when we are learning English. Sadly, a lot of people feel bad when they are learning English. Some are tired. Or they get bored. Do you think you learn English quickly this way? No. Absolutely not. All you have to do is make small changes in your body. First, push your shoulders back. Stand up straight and tall, and if you’re sitting, make your back straight and tall. Bring your chin up. Just by smiling, you can trick your mind into feeling happier. So remember this - every time you listen to these sections, I want you to consciously change your body. Shoulders back. Head up.
